Facial Biometric Setup

Follow these steps to complete your one-time facial registration. This helps us verify your identity during attendance.

  • Ensure you are in a well-lit area.
  • Remove any hats or sunglasses.
  • Look directly at the camera.
  • Fit your face within the oval guide on the screen.
Your biometric data is securely encrypted and will be used solely for attendance verification.
